Wind Energy

Wind is one of Ireland's main sources of energy. It is cheaper then fossil fuel and Ireland has abundance of wind. It would also cut down both the exports from abroad for fossil fuel and the pollution level. However we should vary our energy sources. Ireland cannot rely on wind energy alone and luckily we have access to other resources as well: from thermal energy to hydr energy, Ireland is rich of resources.
